## Big Data Bowl
In the 2025 Big Data Bowl, I had the opportunity to analyze NFL player tracking data to uncover patterns and trends that could inform coaching strategies and player evaluations. My analysis focused on the separation between wide receivers and defensive backs, as I developed a tool and metric to translate that separation into actionable insights, helping defenses anticipate and prepare for the ball in flight. The full project notebook can be found on [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/code/adamkovariu/custom-coverage-lab).

## Personal Projects
### Created a dashboard to visualize player availability and injury impact on team performance for the Green Bay Packers in the 2023 NFL season.
Green Bay Packers Injury Dashboard: In this personal project, I developed a Shiny dashboard in R to visualize player availability and the impact of injuries on team performance for the Green Bay Packers during the 2023 NFL season. This tool helps coaches and analysts understand how injuries affect team dynamics and performance. The player productivity grades were sourced from Pro Football Focus (PFF). The dashboard can be accessed [here](https://adamkovaranalyticsprojects.shinyapps.io/PackersAvailabilityShiny/).
